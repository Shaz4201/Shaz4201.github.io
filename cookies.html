Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how LuxePixel Studios (“we”, “us”, “our”), operating from the UK, uses cookies and similar technologies on our website in compliance with the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR) and the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR).

1. Definitions

  • Cookies: Small text files stored on your device when you visit a website.
  • Essential cookies: Necessary for core site functions (e.g. form submissions).
  • Analytics cookies: Collect anonymous data to help us improve site performance (e.g. Google Analytics).
  • Preference cookies: Remember user choices such as language or display settings.
  • Third-party cookies: Placed by external services (e.g. advertising, analytics providers).

2. Lawful Basis & Consent

Under PECR, we rely on your consent to set any non-essential cookies. You may refuse or withdraw consent at any time via our cookie settings banner or your browser controls. Essential cookies are set on the basis of our legitimate interest in operating the website.

3. Categories & Purpose

  • Essential Cookies: - Enable form submissions and secure site functionality. - Retention: Session only.
  • Analytics Cookies: - Measure site usage and performance (e.g. page views, user flow). - Provided by Google Analytics (Measurement ID: G-0VTJTGHLPF). - Retention: 14 months.
  • Preference Cookies: - Remember your preferences (e.g. language choice). - Retention: 12 months.
  • Third-Party Cookies: - May be set by services we embed (e.g. video players). - Subject to the third party’s own cookie policy.

4. Managing & Withdrawing Consent

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time by clicking the “Cookie Settings” link in the footer or via your browser settings. Blocking or deleting cookies may impair certain features of this site.

5. Changes to This Policy

We review this policy annually. Any updates will be published here with a revised “Effective Date”. We encourage you to revisit this page regularly.

6. Contact Information

For questions or to exercise your data rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at contact@luxepixelstudios.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.
