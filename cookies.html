Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how LuxePixel Studios ("we", "us", "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website. By using our website, you agree to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, phone, tablet) when you visit a website. Cookies allow the website to recognize your device, remember your preferences, and help improve your overall browsing experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the operation of our website, such as logging into your account or submitting a form.
  • Analytics Cookies: We may use cookies to analyze website traffic, track user behavior, and improve our content and services. For example, Google Analytics cookies help us understand how visitors interact with the site.
  • Preference Cookies: These cookies allow us to remember your preferences, such as language settings or personalized content.

3. Managing Cookies

You can manage your cookies settings through your web browser. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies, but please note that doing so may impact your experience on our website. For example, you may not be able to use certain features like logging in or saving preferences.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may also use third-party services that place cookies on our website (e.g., Google Analytics, advertising networks). These third parties may track your usage across multiple websites and may use the data they collect for marketing and analytics purposes.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the updated policy on this page and update the “Effective Date” at the bottom of the page. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any changes.
