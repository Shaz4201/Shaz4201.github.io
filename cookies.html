Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how LuxePixel Studios ("we", "us", "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website. By using our website, you agree to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, phone, tablet) when you visit a website. Cookies allow the website to recognize your device, remember your preferences, and help improve your overall browsing experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for site operation, e.g. logging in or submitting forms.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us analyze traffic and improve content (e.g. Google Analytics).
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings, like language preferences.

3. Managing Cookies

You can manage cookies via your browser settings. Blocking or deleting cookies may affect site features, such as logging in or saved preferences.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services (e.g. advertising networks, analytics) that set their own cookies. These parties may track your browsing across multiple sites.
