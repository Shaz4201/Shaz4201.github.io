Accessibility Statement

LuxePixel Studios is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all users, including those with disabilities. We are actively working to improve the accessibility of our website and ensure we meet relevant standards such as the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA.

What We’re Doing

  • Use clear and simple language where possible
  • Maintain consistent structure and navigation
  • Ensure compatibility with screen readers and assistive technologies
  • Offer a responsive design that works across all devices

Feedback & Contact

If you encounter any accessibility barriers while using our site, please let us know. We value your feedback and will do our best to resolve issues.

Contact: contact@luxepixelstudios.com

Ongoing Commitment

As our site evolves, we will continue to improve accessibility wherever possible and welcome feedback from users to help guide these improvements.
