Accessibility Statement

LuxePixel Studios (“we”, “us”) is committed to ensuring that its digital services are accessible to all users, in accordance with the Equality Act 2010 and Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA.

Scope

This statement applies to all content published on https://luxepixelstudios.com as operated from the United Kingdom.

Standards Compliance

We have adopted WCAG 2.1 Level AA as our target standard for accessibility. Our ongoing programme includes:

  • Semantic HTML markup and ARIA roles to support assistive technologies
  • Consistent, keyboard-navigable interfaces throughout the site
  • Text alternatives for non-text content and meaningful link text
  • Colour contrast ratios meeting at least AA requirements
  • Responsive layouts for mobile, tablet, and desktop devices

Feedback Process

We welcome feedback on the accessibility of our site. To report an issue, please contact us at:

  • Email: contact@luxepixelstudios.com

Upon receipt, we shall:

  • Acknowledge your report within 2 business days
  • Assess and resolve confirmed issues within 10 business days, or provide you with an alternative accessible format where immediate resolution is not feasible

Technical Specifications

Our site is built using modern web technologies, including HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. We employ automated and manual testing tools—such as WAVE, Axe, and screen-reader validation—to continuously monitor accessibility compliance.

Limitations & Alternatives

Despite our efforts, some content may not yet conform fully to WCAG 2.1 AA. Where this occurs, we will provide an accessible alternative (e.g., text transcript for multimedia). Please contact us if you require assistance accessing any content.
